Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese,veggies or meats.



Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes


Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 large eggs.
  • Salt, to taste.
  • Butter, to taste.
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil.
  • Optional fillings: cheese,diced vegetables, cooked meats,herbs.
